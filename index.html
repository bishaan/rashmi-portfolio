About

The researcher
behind the work

I'm a Senior UX Researcher with a Master's in Cognitive Psychology from San Francisco State University and a background in clinical counseling. That combination isn't accidental — the best UX research requires the same skills as good clinical work: deep listening, pattern recognition under pressure, and the ability to distinguish what people say from what they actually do.

I specialize in research that others find hard to do: high-stakes environments, vulnerable populations, and moments where standard self-report methods fail. My work at Action Behavior Centers — where I had to understand clinician behavior in active therapy sessions with children — required observation skills that no survey could replicate.

I'm currently building AI-assisted research frameworks that compress synthesis timelines without sacrificing analytical rigor. My approach: AI accelerates detection, human judgment determines meaning.
